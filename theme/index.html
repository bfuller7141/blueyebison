The Magic Behind Blue Eye Bison's Photography

Hailing from the enchanting landscapes of Northern New Mexico, Wesley's connection with nature and his surroundings has inspired his unique vision. With a commitment to capturing fleeting moments and unveiling the extraordinary in the ordinary, Wesley's work encapsulates the essence of street photography, astrophotography, and landscape photography.

Nature's Mysteries Seen Through Phography

Every click of the shutter is infused with Wesley's genuine love for photography. His keen eye for detail and impeccable timing enable him to freeze time and preserve fleeting emotions, delicate colors, and share breathtaking stories. Everyday becomes extraordinary, the familiar becomes mysterious, and the unnoticed becomes awe-inspiring.
